## Ordering decision rules

Before ordering a part, record:

1. What scanner subsystem it belongs to.
2. Why this part is needed.
3. Required specification.
4. Compatible software/control interface.
5. Safety concern, if any.
6. Alternative part.
7. Final reason for choosing it.

---

## Notes

Do not order actuator, motor driver, DAQ, or sensor parts only by price.

For this project, compatibility, safety, documentation, and testability are more important than the cheapest option.
